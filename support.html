Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

Hold anywhere to rise, release to fall. Thread your dot through the gates and skim the glowing edges without touching them — the closer you graze, the more you score. Touching an edge ends the run.

What is the Heat multiplier?

Chaining grazes builds Heat, raising your score multiplier through four tiers. Playing it safe lets Heat bleed away, so keep skimming the edge to keep it climbing.

What's the difference between Daily and Endless?

Daily is one seeded course that's the same for every player and resets each day — compete for the day's top score. Endless is a procedurally generated course that keeps getting harder.

My leaderboard scores aren't showing.

Leaderboards use Apple Game Center. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in your device Settings and connected to the internet. The app is fully playable without Game Center, but scores won't sync globally.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

No. GRAZE is a one-time purchase with no ads, no timers, and no in-app purchases. Death, then instant retry — that's the whole loop.
