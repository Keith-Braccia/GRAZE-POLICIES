GRAZE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

GRAZE ("the app") is a single-player arcade game. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle. In short: the developer does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information.

Information we collect

The developer operates no servers and collects no personal data. The app has no accounts, no registration, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking software.

Data stored on your device

The app saves a small amount of information locally on your device only:

  • The player initials you enter (for the on-device scoreboard).
  • Your best scores and recent results.

This data never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Apple Game Center, the app submits your scores to Game Center leaderboards so you can compare results with other players. Your Game Center alias and scores are handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy, not by the developer. Game Center is optional — the app is fully playable without signing in. You can manage or disable Game Center in your device Settings.

Haptics and sound

The app uses the device's Taptic Engine (vibration) and plays sound effects. These features do not collect any information.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
